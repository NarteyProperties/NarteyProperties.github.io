About Us

Short Biography

Nartey Properties is a Ghanaian registered Real Estate with the Registrar General Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs And The Ghana Supremecourt.


Vision

To help Ghanaians abroad build their dream home. Eliminating the middle man.
Nartey Properties can procure land, provide a building plan and oversee the building of your dream home while you are earning and living outside of Ghana. You can use our architectural drawings or submit drawings of your own. Once plans have been finalized we will give an estimate for the completion or the home. The project will be done in quarterly statements and payments. During the Quartely communications, pictures of the building progress will be submitted and changes you deem necessary will be discussed and implemented. Any and all adjustments will be provided in writing throughout the building project. Agents will make themselves accessible for all your needs and concerns. You would be welcome to view your home in progress at any time either in person or on SKYPE to make you a part of your own project.
